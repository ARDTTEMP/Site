About BRAC

BRAC is a global leader in developing cost-effective, evidence-based programs in conflict-prone and post-disaster settings. These programs are designed to empower people living in poverty to achieve their potential.

Founded in Bangladesh in 1972, BRAC operates across Asia and Africa, touching the lives of over 100 million people. Our holistic approach combines microfinance, education, healthcare, and livelihood support to create opportunities for the ultra-poor.

Our Programs

BRAC implements integrated development programs in education, healthcare, livelihoods, and social empowerment to create lasting change.

Providing quality education to children who are out of school or at risk of dropping out through our primary and pre-primary schools.
