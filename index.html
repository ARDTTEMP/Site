Our Approach

BRAC takes a holistic approach to alleviating poverty by addressing its many dimensions through integrated, community-based programs.

Ultra-Poor Graduation

A comprehensive approach to help the poorest households build sustainable livelihoods and break the cycle of poverty.

Education

Providing quality education to children who are out of school or at risk of dropping out through innovative models.

Microfinance

Offering financial services to empower people, especially women, to build businesses and improve their lives.

Featured Story: Empowering Women in Bangladesh

Meet Rina, a woman who transformed her life through BRAC's Ultra-Poor Graduation program. With training, assets, and ongoing support, she built a successful poultry business that now supports her entire family.
